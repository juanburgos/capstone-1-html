  1. Music

    2. It's hard to say that I have a favorite kind of music or a favorite band or artist. There's music for every moment, activity or sentiment. As a musician, I like to explore every genre of music.

    This does not mean that I will like everything that I listen to by default. Still, I will use judgement to determine if whatever I am listening to is worth my time and effort.

    For example, I look for the quality of the production. I'll pay attention to the instrumentation and musicianship, harmonies, lyrics, recording quality, album art, and originality.

    Not trying to sound anything like snobby here. But I want you to maybe give me the benefit-of-doubt that I just pay close attention to good quality, and that is what I prefer to consume.
